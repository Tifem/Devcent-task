The President's Welcome Speech

Welcome to ICAN Abeokuta & District Society Chapter's website. As President of ICAN Nigeria, I
commend your dedication to excellence in acountancy. This platform offers a wealth of resources for
professional growth and networking. Let's collaborate and innovate our profession
and serve society. Thank you for your commitment to upholding the highest standards of integrity and
professionalism. Together, we will make a lasting impact in the field of accountancy.

DR. INNOCENT IWEKA OKWUOSA, (PhD, CPFA, FCIB, FCA)
President, ICAN
